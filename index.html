About Us

We are dedicated to showcasing chord progressions of old Japanese pop songs. Our site is a valuable resource for users who know the songs but may not understand Japanese and have no way of searching for them. Even those who are not interested in chord progressions or playing music can use the site to explore an artist's discography.
